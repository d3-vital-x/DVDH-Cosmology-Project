\section{Lagrangian and Action Formulation}

We define the full DVDH action as:

\[
S = \int d^4x \sqrt{-g} \left[ R + \mathcal{L}_{VX} + 
\mathcal{L}_{H} + \mathcal{L}_{\gamma} \right]
\]

Key elements include:

- Vital Field Lagrangian $\mathcal{L}_{VX}$
- Mass-cancellation interaction
- Photon energy-dilution term
- Higgs–vacuum drift contribution
- Dimensional deviation $\Delta D$ terms

The Euler–Lagrange variations yield the collapse equation and background
evolution equations.
