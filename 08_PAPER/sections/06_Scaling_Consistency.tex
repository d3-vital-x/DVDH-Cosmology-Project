\section{Scaling Consistency and Microphysical Derivation}

This chapter shows how the microscopic DSI model generates the effective
cosmological scaling parameters $\alpha$ and $\epsilon$.

The mapping is:

\[
\alpha \sim \frac{DSI_{\text{mean}}}{DSI_{\max}},
\qquad
\epsilon \sim \frac{R_{\text{collapse}}}{T_{\text{collapse}}}
\]

Results support the scaling structure required for DVDH cosmology.
